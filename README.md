E-commerce platforms provide a convenient way for businesses to reach a larger audience and for customers to compare products, make payments, and receive products at their doorstep.

Some common types of e-commerce include:

Business-to-Consumer (B2C): Businesses sell directly to customers.
Business-to-Business (B2B): Businesses sell products or services to other businesses.
Consumer-to-Consumer (C2C): Individuals sell products to other individuals through online marketplaces.
Consumer-to-Business (C2B): Individuals provide products or services to businesses.

Popular e-commerce companies include Amazon, Flipkart, and eBay.

Advantages of E-commerce:

24/7 shopping availability
Wide variety of products
Easy price comparison
Convenient online payment options
Global market reach

E-commerce has transformed the way people shop and conduct business, making transactions faster, easier, and more accessible.
